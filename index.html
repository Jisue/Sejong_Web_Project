About Software Department

Software will play an important role in the fourth industrial revolution industry. AI will be integrated to create a virtual physics system that can control things automatically and intelligently, and state-of-the-art technologies will emerge in the future. Today's society has a growing demand for people who can handle advanced software. Therefore, Sejong University established the Software Department in 2017 to foster professional programmers who deal with high-level software.

Through appropriate curriculum, the Department of Software will provide a platform for active activities in various fields of computer application.
